The Iron Golem is a formidable creature often encountered in various fantasy settings, particularly within the realms of role-playing games, literature, and folklore. It is typically depicted as a massive humanoid construct crafted entirely from iron or other metals, animated through powerful magic or advanced technology. The Iron Golem's primary purpose is often that of a guardian or protector, tasked with defending valuable treasures, ancient ruins, or powerful wizards' strongholds.
